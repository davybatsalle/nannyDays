Welcome to NannyDays, an Android app designed to help childminders to track
their working days and hours, and to manage their clients.
This document outlines the requirements for the app, including functional and
non-functional requirements.

## Functional Requirements

1. Child profiles
   - The app should allow childminders to create and manage profiles for each child they care for.
   - Each profile should include the child's name, age, and standard hours of
     care.
   - Each profile should allow the childminder to generate a QR code that can be
     scanned by the childminder to quickly log in and out of care sessions for that child.
  
   
2. Time tracking
   - The app should allow childminders to track their working hours for each child and client.
   - The app should provide a way to log the start and end times of care
     sessions, either manually or by scanning the child's QR code on a printed
     card.
    - The app should calculate the total hours worked for each child and client,
      and provide a summary of hours worked over a specified period.
    

3. Reporting
   - The app should provide a reporting feature that allows childminders to
     generate reports of their working hours per children
   - The app should allow childminders to export reports in a common format
     (e.g., CSV or PDF) for record-keeping and sharing with parents.
   
## Non-Functional Requirements
1. Usability
   - The app should have an intuitive and user-friendly interface that is easy to navigate for childminders of all ages and technical skill levels.
   - The app should provide clear instructions and feedback to guide users
     through the process of creating profiles, logging hours, and generating
     reports.
2. Performance
    - The app should load quickly and respond promptly to user interactions, ensuring a smooth user experience.
    - The app should be optimized for performance, minimizing battery usage and
      data consumption.

3. Compatibility
    - The app should be compatible with a wide range of Android devices and versions, ensuring accessibility for all users.
    - The app should be regularly updated to maintain compatibility with new
      Android versions and devices.

## User Stories
1. As a childminder, I want to create profiles for each child I care for, so that I can easily manage their information and track my working hours.
2. As a childminder, I want to log my working hours for each child, so that I
   can keep accurate records of my care sessions.
3. As a childminder, I want to generate reports of my working hours, so that I can share them with parents and keep track of my earnings.
4. As a childminder, I want to export my reports in a common format, so that I can easily share them with parents and keep them for my records.
5. As a childminder, I want to have a user-friendly interface, so that I can easily navigate the app and use its features without confusion.
6. As a childminder, I want the app to perform well and load quickly, so that I can use it efficiently without delays.
7. As a childminder, I want the app to be compatible with my Android device, so
   that I can use it without any technical issues.
